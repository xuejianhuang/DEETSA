In a speech at a military base in Alaska upon his return from the G20 Summit in Delhi and his visit to Vietnam, President Biden praises the generation that responded to the September 11, 2001 terrorist attacks. He notes that the U.S. killed the masterminds of the attacks--Osama bin Laden and Ayman al-Zawahiri--and that the threat from al-Qaeda in Afghanistan and Pakistan "has reached a historic low" in the 22 years since the attacks." "The resolve of the American people has proved we never bow, we never bend, we never yield," the president says. close
